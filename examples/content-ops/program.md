# Content Operations Team

## Mission
Research trending topics in AI/crypto/growth and produce short-form content (LinkedIn posts, Twitter threads) that practitioners find genuinely useful.

## Constraints
- Every piece must include specific data, numbers, or examples -- no vague thought leadership
- Practitioner voice: sounds like someone who built the thing, not someone selling it
- Each output should be self-contained and actionable within 2 minutes of reading

## Content Standards
- Hook must create pattern interrupt in the first line
- Include at least one specific metric, example, or contrarian take
- End with a clear takeaway, not a question or CTA

# Email Operations Team

## Mission
Write high-performing email campaigns by treating every send as an experiment. Test subject lines, copy structures, CTAs, and personalization approaches systematically. Optimize for opens first (subject line), then clicks (body copy), then conversions (CTA and landing page alignment).

## How It Works
The researcher analyzes past campaign performance and identifies which patterns are working or failing. The copywriter produces email body copy informed by those patterns. The subject line tester generates 5-8 subject line variants optimized for open rate, applying lessons from previous A/B tests.

## Constraints
- Every email campaign must include at least 3 subject line variants for A/B testing.
- Track and learn from every send: what opened, what clicked, what converted.
- No clickbait subject lines that don't match the email body. Opens without clicks = broken trust.
- Keep emails scannable: 150 words max for promotional, 300 words max for educational.
- One clear CTA per email. Multiple CTAs split attention and kill click-through.

# Quality Lab Team

## Mission
Cross-team quality auditor. Reads outputs from ALL teams via the shared knowledge store
and evaluates them against quality standards. Identifies patterns in what works and what doesn't.

## What Makes This Team Different
This team doesn't produce content -- it produces INSIGHT. It reads other teams' work,
scores it, and identifies systemic issues. Its knowledge artifacts feed back into
other teams' context on the next cycle.

## Quality Framework
Score each output on:
1. Specificity (0-10): Does it include concrete numbers, examples, data?
2. Actionability (0-10): Can someone DO something with this in 2 minutes?
3. Voice (0-10): Does it sound like a practitioner, not a marketer?
4. Hook strength (0-10): Would you stop scrolling for this?

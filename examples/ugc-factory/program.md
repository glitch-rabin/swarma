# UGC Factory

## Mission

Produce AI-generated UGC-style video ads that look and perform like human creator content -- at 90-98% cost reduction. $2-20/video vs $150-2K for human creators. Test hook variants at volume, rotate creative every 2-3 weeks, maintain ROAS.

## How This Actually Works in 2026

Product URL -> AI scrapes product data -> LLM writes UGC-style scripts (testimonial, problem-solution, unboxing) -> AI avatar selection matched to target demo -> voice synthesis with lip-sync -> video assembly with platform-native formatting -> 20-50 hook variants per body -> batch export in all formats -> launch all variants -> 48h performance window -> kill bottom 80%.

AI UGC performs within 10-20% of human creator content on ROAS for short-form ads under 60 seconds. MakeUGC reports 3.1x average ROAS across brand portfolio. Arcads case studies show $130K+/month revenue through AI UGC campaigns.

EU AI Act (2026): mandatory labeling of AI-generated content. Deepfake/synthetic media disclosure required. Build disclosure into every video.

## Experiment Patterns

1. **AI avatar vs human creator**: same script, same product, same audience -- A/B test ROAS
2. **Hook variant volume**: 10 hooks vs 50 hooks -- measure probability of finding a winner
3. **Avatar demographic matching**: match avatar to target audience vs mismatch -- measure engagement
4. **Script style**: testimonial vs problem-solution vs educational vs emotional -- measure CPA
5. **Refresh cadence**: new creative weekly vs bi-weekly vs monthly -- measure fatigue curves
6. **Language expansion**: same top performer dubbed in 5 languages -- measure international ROAS
7. **Platform adaptation**: TikTok-native vs Instagram-native vs Facebook-native style

## Metrics

- **Primary**: hook_rate (% viewers past 3 seconds, target: >30%)
- **Revenue**: ROAS per creative variant
- **Cost**: CPA per variant, cost_per_video ($2-20 target)
- **Durability**: creative_lifespan (days until >20% performance degradation)
- **Win rate**: % of generated variants that outperform control

## Constraints

- EU AI Act compliance: every AI-generated video must include synthetic media disclosure
- Rotate new UGC every 2-3 weeks for sustained ROAS
- Avatar selection must match target demographic for authenticity
- No variant goes live without a control baseline to test against
- Kill threshold: bottom 80% of variants killed within 48 hours

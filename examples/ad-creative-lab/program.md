# Ad Creative Lab

## Mission

Generate, test, and iterate ad creative at scale. The teams that test fastest win -- 50+ variants per campaign, 48-hour performance windows, kill bottom 80%, scale winners. Every cycle produces better generation prompts.

## How This Actually Works in 2026

The pipeline: brief ingestion -> variant generation (20-100 creatives) -> format adaptation per platform -> bulk launch -> 24-48h data collection -> kill/scale -> winning elements feed next generation cycle.

61% of brand/agency marketers already use AI for programmatic advertising. Uber + Smartly.io saw +108% CTR, -43% CPC, -36% CPA. Smartly.io testing: 45.7% lift across 10 tests, 9/10 tests showed improvement.

This squad handles the ideation and copy layer. Pair with AdCreative.ai, Pencil, or Creatify for image/video generation, and Meta Advantage+/Google PMax for algorithmic distribution.

## Experiment Patterns

1. **Hook taxonomy**: test 5 hook archetypes (question, stat, contrarian, pain point, social proof) across same product -- measure CTR
2. **AI vs human creative**: same brief to AI and human designer -- A/B test on identical audiences
3. **Volume threshold**: 10 vs 50 vs 200 variants -- at what point do more variants stop improving results?
4. **Refresh cadence**: new creative weekly vs bi-weekly vs monthly -- measure fatigue curves
5. **Platform-native**: same concept adapted per platform vs universal creative -- measure platform-specific ROAS
6. **Dynamic vs static**: catalog-fed dynamic variants vs static AI-generated -- measure for e-commerce

## Metrics

- **Primary**: creative_win_rate (% of variants that outperform control)
- **Speed**: time_to_first_variant (hours from brief to live ad)
- **Cost**: cost_per_variant (generation cost / usable variants)
- **Durability**: creative_fatigue_rate (days until performance degrades)
- **Revenue**: ROAS by variant type

## Constraints

- Every variant must map to one testable hypothesis (hook type, CTA style, visual approach)
- Brand guidelines enforced: colors, fonts, tone, logo placement
- Platform specs respected: 1:1 Instagram, 9:16 Stories/Reels, 16:9 YouTube
- No variant goes live without a clear baseline to test against
- Kill threshold: bottom 80% of variants killed within 48 hours

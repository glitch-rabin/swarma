# Crypto Research Team

## Mission
Daily DeFi market intelligence combining on-chain data (TVL flows, liquidation cascades, whale movements) with protocol-level analysis and macro context. The output should be actionable for both content creation and trading decisions.

## How It Works
The data collector pulls fresh on-chain metrics via DeFiLlama, CoinGecko, and protocol-specific APIs. The analyst identifies anomalies, divergences, and patterns that signal opportunity or risk. The strategist translates analysis into specific action items: trade setups worth watching, protocols worth covering in content, and risk alerts.

## Constraints
- Every claim must reference specific on-chain data. No vibes-based analysis.
- Distinguish between observation (this happened), inference (this likely means), and speculation (this could lead to).
- Include risk assessment for every opportunity. What's the thesis-killer?
- Track prediction accuracy: every actionable call gets a 7-day follow-up score.
- Never recommend specific trades. Surface setups and let humans decide.

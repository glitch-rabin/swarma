# Market Intelligence Team

## Mission
Monitor competitors, adjacent players, and the broader market for signals that matter -- product launches, pricing changes, hiring patterns, funding rounds, strategic pivots. Deliver a daily briefing that tells you what happened, what it means, and what to do about it.

## How It Works
The scanner casts a wide net across product pages, social media, job boards, and press. The analyst filters noise from signal and identifies patterns across multiple data points. The briefer synthesizes everything into a 2-minute read with clear action items ranked by urgency.

## Constraints
- Briefing must be under 500 words. If it takes more than 2 minutes to read, it's too long.
- Every insight needs a "so what" -- what should we do differently because of this?
- Distinguish between confirmed facts and inferred signals. Label speculation clearly.
- Track competitor moves over time, not just point-in-time snapshots.
- If nothing meaningful happened, the briefing should say "quiet day" and explain why the silence itself might be notable.

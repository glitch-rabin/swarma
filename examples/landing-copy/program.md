# Landing Copy Team

## Mission
Produce high-converting landing page copy through a structured research-write-critique cycle. Every element -- headline, subhead, body, CTA, social proof placement -- is treated as a testable hypothesis. The goal is conversion clarity: does the visitor immediately understand what this is, why it matters, and what to do next?

## How It Works
The researcher analyzes the target audience, competitive positioning, and existing conversion data to produce a positioning brief. The copywriter drafts landing page copy against that brief. The critic scores the copy on conversion psychology principles and produces a revised version with specific improvements.

## Constraints
- Hero section must pass the "5 second test": a visitor should understand the product and value prop within 5 seconds.
- One primary CTA per page. Secondary CTAs must be clearly subordinate.
- Every claim needs proof: numbers, testimonials, logos, or demonstrations.
- Copy must work without images/design -- if the words aren't clear in a Google Doc, they won't be clear on the page.
- No jargon the target audience wouldn't use themselves.

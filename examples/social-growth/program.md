# Social Growth Team

## Mission
Research one high-signal topic daily and distribute platform-native content across LinkedIn, Twitter, and (optionally) newsletter. Each platform gets a version tuned to its algorithm and audience expectations -- not a copy-paste.

## How It Works
The researcher surfaces one topic with specific data and a contrarian angle. The growth lead decides which platforms to target and writes a distribution brief with platform-specific hooks. Writers execute against those briefs in parallel, each optimizing for their platform's algorithm (LinkedIn favors depth + saves, Twitter favors replies + QTs).

## Constraints
- Never publish identical content across platforms. Same insight, different packaging.
- LinkedIn posts max 200 words, Twitter max 280 chars (or short thread if warranted).
- Every piece must include at least one specific number or data point.
- No engagement farming ("what do you think?", polls, emoji spam).

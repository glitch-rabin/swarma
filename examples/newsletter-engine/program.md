# Newsletter Engine

## Mission

Build a newsletter growth and monetization system. AI handles 80% of the work (curation, drafting, segmentation, personalization). Humans provide editorial direction and taste. Revenue per subscriber per month is the north star metric.

## How This Actually Works in 2026

The stack: AI scans RSS/Twitter/Reddit for stories -> filters by relevance -> drafts summaries and analysis -> human spends 15-30 min on editorial pass -> beehiiv segments and personalizes -> automated send at optimal time -> referral program drives growth -> sponsorships + programmatic + subscriptions monetize.

Revenue benchmarks: 1K subscribers = $3-5K/month (heavy digital products + affiliate). 10K = $10-30K/month (sponsorships viable). 50K+ = $50-200K/month (Lenny's Newsletter model). AI automation saves ~75 hours/month in creation time.

Platform choice: beehiiv (built by ex-Morning Brew team, best monetization tools, 0% revenue cut) or Kit/ConvertKit (better for existing digital product business).

## Experiment Patterns

1. **Curation depth**: AI-summarized links vs AI-written analysis vs human hot takes -- measure click rate
2. **Send frequency**: daily vs 3x/week vs weekly -- measure open rate and churn tradeoff
3. **Subject line**: AI-generated A/B vs human-written -- measure open rate
4. **Segment-specific content**: personalized blocks per segment vs one-size-fits-all -- measure click rate
5. **Monetization mix**: heavy sponsorship vs heavy affiliate vs paid tier push -- measure RPM
6. **Referral incentive**: free month vs exclusive content vs merch -- measure viral coefficient

## Metrics

- **Primary**: revenue_per_subscriber_per_month (blend all revenue streams)
- **Growth**: list_growth_rate (target: 5-10% monthly), referral_rate
- **Engagement**: open_rate (target: >45%), click_rate (target: >5%)
- **Retention**: churn_rate (target: <2% monthly)
- **Monetization**: sponsor_renewal_rate, RPM per send

## Constraints

- Editorial pass is non-negotiable. AI curates and drafts, human adds taste.
- Open rate <40% = diagnose immediately (subject lines, send time, list hygiene)
- Churn >3% = content-audience mismatch. Re-evaluate niche or format.
- Every send must have at least one monetization touchpoint (sponsor, affiliate, CTA)
- Segment before you scale. One-size-fits-all dies at 5K+ subscribers.

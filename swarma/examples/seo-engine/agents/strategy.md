# SEO Strategy

## Seed Knowledge (pre-experiment baseline)

### Validated Patterns

**Topical authority > individual page optimization**
- 20 interlinked articles on one topic outrank 20 scattered articles
- Pillar page + cluster model: one hub (3000+ words) linked to 8-15 supporting pages
- Internal linking between cluster pages signals depth to search engines

**Search intent matching**
- Informational ("how to X") = long-form guide, list, tutorial
- Commercial ("best X for Y") = comparison, review, listicle
- Transactional ("buy X") = product page, pricing page
- Mismatched intent = zero chance of ranking regardless of quality

**Content structure signals**
- H2/H3 hierarchy matching search queries improves featured snippet capture
- FAQ sections at bottom capture long-tail "people also ask" queries
- Table of contents with jump links reduces bounce rate
- 1500-2500 words is the sweet spot for most informational queries

**Programmatic SEO patterns**
- Template + data = thousands of pages (city pages, tool comparisons, industry pages)
- Each page needs unique value, not just swapped variables
- Internal linking mesh between programmatic pages is critical
- Thin programmatic pages get penalized. Quality floor: would a human bookmark this?

### Patterns to Test

- [ ] Original research/data posts vs curated roundup posts for backlinks
- [ ] Update frequency: monthly refreshes vs publish-and-forget
- [ ] Long-form (3000+) vs medium-form (1500) for ranking velocity
- [ ] Author entity (named expert author) vs brand-only byline
